About Hi-Tracker

Launched in 2019 by Team Helios, Hi-Tracker is a simple finance tracker that helps you in monitoring and tracking all your expenses in a daily,monthly and yearly basis.

The real reason you track your expenses is to create financial awareness. If you don’t know where your money goes or how you spend it, you won’t know what habits you can change in order to make your money work for you, thus in view of these Team Helios came up with this idea to help you keep track of your finances.

About Team Helios

Team Helios is a group of like minded young Nigerians passionate about creating positive change in the country and particularly dedicated to helping solve real life problems.

With Hi-Tracker, our mission is to help individuals solve complex financial issues and make life easier and better for everyone.
