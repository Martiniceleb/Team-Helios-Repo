How The Calculator Works

When you fill the calculator form by inputing the power ratings of your equipments, the system will multiply the power rating data you provided for each equipment by their respective number of working hours.

The result is summed up and depending on your location, a multiplier is applied which is a function of the possible hours of sunshine available in a day within your location.

With the result, we are able to recommend the power rating of the solar panel you require to power your home appliances.
