ABOUT TEAM HELIOS FINANCE TRACKER

Tracking Expenses Creates Financial Awareness

Launched in 2019, Team helios finance tracker is a simple finance tracker that helps you in monitoring and tracking all your expenses in a daily,monthly and yearly basis.The real reason you track your expenses is to create financial awareness. If you don’t know where your money goes or how you spend it, you won’t know what habits you can change in order to make your money work for you, thus in view of these Team Helios came up with this idea to help you keep track of your finances.Team Helios is a group of like minded young Nigerians passionate about creating positive change in the country and particularly dedicated to helping solve real life problems.

Our Mission

To help individuals solve complex financial issues and make life easier and better for everyone.

Our Work

We are here to help families resolve the following financial issues
Record every purchase, regardless of the amount spent
Stick to Your Budget
Helps Identify Spending Issues
Keep Going Even When You Overspend and many others.
